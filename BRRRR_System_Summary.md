## What's Been Built

You now have a complete BRRRR investment system with three interconnected tools:

### 1️⃣ BRRRR Deal Tracker (Notion Database)
**Purpose:** Track individual properties through the entire BRRRR cycle

**URL:** https://www.notion.so/936926d8f35342e1a43c4bb66fc04e34

**Features:**
- 40+ fields covering: Buy, Rehab, Rent, Refinance, Repeat
- Auto-calculated metrics: Cash-on-Cash Return, Cap Rate, Annual Cash Flow, Monthly Cash Flow, Break-Even Timeline
- 8 deal statuses: Lead → Negotiating → Under Contract → Closed → Rehab → Renting → Refinanced → Repeat Ready
- Contact tracking: Wholesalers, contractors, lenders, property managers
- Document attachment: Inspection reports, contracts, estimates
- Color-coded property conditions and deal statuses

### 2️⃣ Market Analysis Tracker (Notion Database)
**Purpose:** Understand local market conditions to validate deals

**URL:** https://www.notion.so/2e33ce7a0b4f49d693d8c76b4acec2ea

**Features:**
- 4 entry types: Market Overview, Neighborhood Deep Dive, Comparable Sales, Market Trends
- 50+ fields covering: Market metrics, rental data, growth rates, contractor costs, wholesaler info
- Color-coded selections: Market trends, rental demand, investment opportunity level
- Benchmark metrics: Average cap rates, cash-on-cash returns, appreciation rates

### 3️⃣ Market Automation Script (Python)
**Purpose:** Automatically populate market data to keep it current

**Files:** 
- `brrrr_market_automation.py` - The main automation script
- `BRRRR_Setup_Guide.md` - Complete setup and usage guide

**Features:**
- Fetches data from Zillow, county assessor records
- Calculates derived metrics (cap rates, ROI, appreciation)
- Auto-creates Notion entries
- Can be scheduled for monthly updates
